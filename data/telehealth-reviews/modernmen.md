## How ModernMen Works

The process starts with a $125 one-time initial consultation that covers a health intake, an in-home lab draw, and a same-day results review with a provider. That last piece is notable - most telehealth TRT services have you wait several days for lab results before scheduling a follow-up call. ModernMen turns that around the same day.

After the intake, you move to a monthly membership matched to your goals: testosterone therapy, medical weight loss, or peptides. Your provider continues coming to you for follow-up visits, which is the core premise of the concierge model. Medications are shipped directly, so you're not driving anywhere to pick anything up.

Consultations are synchronous - you talk to a provider live rather than submitting an asynchronous intake form and waiting for a written response. For a service built around in-person visits, that's expected and appreciated.

## Pricing

ModernMen publishes its pricing directly, which is worth noting in a category where many services make you sit through an intake before revealing what you'll pay.

The testosterone therapy membership is $229 per month and includes your hormone panel, in-home visits, the testosterone medication itself, and ongoing protocol management. That's an all-in number - no separate lab bill, no per-visit charge stacked on top. For a concierge model, that's competitive: plenty of telehealth TRT providers charge $150-200 per month for medication alone and bill labs separately.

Medical weight loss runs $349 per month, covering the GLP-1 medication, in-home visits, metabolic labs, and dose management. GLP-1 pricing swings wildly at other services depending on dose and which compounding pharmacy is involved, so a flat monthly number is easier to budget around.

Peptide therapy is $249 per month, covering protocol selection, administration guidance, monitoring, and adjustments.

Whether FSA or HSA cards are accepted isn't disclosed on the site.

## What's Included

The testosterone membership bundles what typically gets billed separately elsewhere - labs, provider visits, and medication - into a single monthly fee. That makes the pricing easier to evaluate honestly: what you see is what you pay.

Beyond standard testosterone injections, ModernMen offers oral testosterone, peptides, IV therapy, PRP, and shockwave therapy. That's a broader treatment menu than most telehealth-only TRT services, which usually stick to injectables and topicals. The in-home setup makes some of those treatments more practical to deliver - IV therapy, for example, isn't something you self-administer.

Labs are included, with same-day results during the initial consultation. Ongoing monitoring specifics aren't fully spelled out publicly, but the monthly membership description includes the hormone panel as part of the recurring service.

## Pros and Cons

The main draw here is the concierge model itself. If you've grown tired of clinic waiting rooms or the fragmented experience of telehealth portals - order labs here, schedule a video call there, receive a shipment somewhere else - having a provider show up at your door is a meaningfully different kind of service. Same-day lab results cut the anxious waiting that comes with most TRT intake processes, and bundled pricing removes the guesswork from monthly costs.

The hard limitation is geography. ModernMen currently serves only the Coachella Valley - Palm Springs and the surrounding desert cities in southern California. If you're not in that footprint, there's nothing here for you regardless of how the model sounds.

The founding-phase status is also worth sitting with. Early-stage services can actually be an advantage - you often get tighter communication and more direct provider access. But it also means the operational patterns are still being established, and the level of polish you'd expect from a mature service may not be there yet. No medical director is listed on the site, and the prescribing model isn't described publicly - for a service handling a Schedule III controlled substance, more visible credentialing would help build confidence.

## Bottom Line

ModernMen is a concierge TRT and men's health service with transparent pricing, a genuinely different delivery model, and a wider treatment menu than most telehealth competitors. For someone in the Coachella Valley who wants an at-home experience and isn't interested in managing lab orders and pharmacy shipments separately, it's a reasonable option to consider. Just go in knowing it's pre-launch, geography-locked, and light on published provider credentials for now.
