## How Age Rejuvenation Works

Age Rejuvenation is a Florida-based hormone and wellness clinic that has been operating for over two decades, treating more than 92,000 patients across its physical locations and, more recently, through virtual telehealth. The company spans functional medicine, hormone optimization, weight management, and aesthetic services - it is a broad wellness practice that includes TRT rather than a TRT-only provider. If you are in Florida or North Carolina and want a clinic with deep roots and in-person options alongside telehealth, it is worth a look. If you are shopping for a stripped-down, TRT-specific telehealth service, the wider menu may feel like more than you need.

## Pricing

Public pricing is not disclosed on the Age Rejuvenation website. You will need to go through their intake process to get a quote. Given the breadth of their services and the functional medicine framing, pricing likely varies considerably depending on what the evaluation turns up and which protocol you land on. Factor that uncertainty into your planning - you are not going to walk in with a number in your head and confirm it on the homepage.

## What's Included

Age Rejuvenation offers four delivery formats for testosterone therapy: injections, topical cream, pellets, and oral testosterone. That is a wider range than many telehealth-first competitors, which tend to stick to injections and sometimes cream. Pellets in particular are less common in the telehealth space - they require an in-office procedure, which Age Rejuvenation can accommodate given its physical locations in Florida.

Because the company takes a functional medicine approach, evaluations tend to go broader than testosterone in isolation. Expect conversations about thyroid, metabolic health, and other hormones alongside a standard testosterone panel. Whether that depth is appealing or feels like upselling depends on what you are after.

Details on whether labs are included in a base fee, whether they ship medications directly to your door, and whether FSA/HSA payments are accepted are not confirmed from public data. Ask specifically about these when you contact them.

## Pros and Cons

The strongest argument for Age Rejuvenation is track record. Twenty-plus years in business, 92,000 patients, and over 1,500 five-star reviews is a meaningful signal in a market crowded with startups that have been around for three years and a logo. The combination of physical locations and telehealth also gives patients more flexibility than a purely virtual provider - if you want to meet a provider in person at some point, or if pellet therapy interests you, that option exists.

The tradeoffs are real, though. Because Age Rejuvenation is a general wellness and medspa clinic rather than a TRT-focused practice, the experience is not built around the streamlined testosterone optimization workflow you would find at a dedicated men's health telehealth company. The breadth of services can mean more friction in the intake process, and the lack of any public pricing means you are committing time to an evaluation before you know whether it fits your budget. The clinic also currently serves only Florida and North Carolina via telehealth, which cuts out most of the country.

## Bottom Line

Age Rejuvenation is a solid option for men in Florida or North Carolina who want a provider with genuine clinical history and the flexibility of both in-person and virtual care. The functional medicine approach and multiple delivery options - including pellets - give it some real differentiation. The opaque pricing and limited telehealth footprint are the main friction points. If you are in a covered state and willing to go through the intake process before knowing cost, the clinic's longevity and patient volume suggest they are doing something right.
