## How Alpha MD Works

Alpha MD is a telehealth men's health clinic offering TRT and hormone optimization entirely online. The pitch is straightforward: fill out an intake, get a lab kit shipped to your door, consult with a provider, and receive your medication by mail. If you're the kind of person who'd rather handle everything from a browser than sit in a waiting room, that's the model.

The process starts with an at-home hormone testing kit, which Alpha MD ships to you at no extra charge - that's a meaningful difference from platforms that ask you to go find a LabCorp or Quest location yourself. Once your labs come back, you have a synchronous consultation with a clinician (a live video or phone call, not an asynchronous message thread). Medical director Saba Haq MD oversees the clinical operation. If you're prescribed treatment, medications and supplies ship directly to you.

Alpha MD offers three delivery formats: injection, cream, and oral testosterone. Having all three available through a single telehealth platform is less common than it sounds - many online TRT providers stick to injections or a single alternative. Whether you're already familiar with one method or want to discuss options with a provider, you're not forced into a one-size-fits-all protocol.

## Pricing

Alpha MD's pricing structure is tiered by commitment length. Monthly billing runs $129/month. If you pay every six months, that drops to $109/month. The annual plan brings it down further to $98/month, which works out to $1,176 paid upfront for the year.

That range - $98 to $129 per month - is competitive for an all-in telehealth TRT service, especially since medications and supplies are bundled in. Many providers charge separately for the medication itself, which can push the real monthly cost well above whatever headline number they advertise. With Alpha MD, what you see appears to be what you pay.

One caveat: there's a minimum three-month commitment required. You won't be able to test the service for a single month and walk away. That's worth knowing before you hand over a credit card.

Alpha MD also offers a 20% discount for active military and veterans. State coverage isn't disclosed publicly - you'd need to complete the intake to confirm availability in your state.

Insurance acceptance isn't confirmed on public-facing pages, and FSA/HSA eligibility isn't addressed either. If those matter to your decision, contact Alpha MD directly before starting.

## What's Included

At each pricing tier, Alpha MD includes the treatment plan, medications, and supplies in the stated monthly cost. The at-home hormone testing kit is included upfront, which covers the lab work needed to establish a baseline before prescribing. Supplies - meaning syringes, alcohol wipes, and whatever else your delivery method requires - are bundled in as well.

The consultation is synchronous, meaning you'll speak with a provider directly rather than going back and forth through a patient portal. That's a better setup for first-time TRT patients who may have questions that don't translate well to async messaging.

## Pros and Cons

The strongest case for Alpha MD is the pricing transparency combined with the all-in cost structure. You're not going to get hit with a separate pharmacy bill at the end of the month. The free at-home lab kit removes a real barrier for people who don't want to schedule a blood draw somewhere, and the military/veteran discount is a concrete benefit that's easy to verify.

The treatment format variety - injections, cream, and oral - gives patients real options, which is worth something when many telehealth TRT services quietly funnel everyone toward the cheapest delivery method.

On the downside, the required minimum commitment means Alpha MD isn't ideal if you're still deciding whether TRT is right for you and want maximum flexibility. The absence of any clear information about insurance or FSA/HSA isn't unusual for this category of provider, but it's still a gap. And without knowing which states Alpha MD serves, it's hard to evaluate the service if you're outside a major metro area.

## Bottom Line

Alpha MD is a clean, well-priced telehealth TRT option for men who want a fully remote experience without hidden costs. The at-home lab kit, bundled medications, synchronous consultations, and multiple treatment formats make it a reasonably complete package in the $98-$129/month range. The three-month minimum is a meaningful commitment for a service you haven't tried yet, and state availability is opaque - so confirm coverage before you go through intake. Testosterone is a Schedule III controlled substance, and any legitimate provider will require a proper evaluation before prescribing.
