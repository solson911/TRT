## How NovaGenix works

The process opens with a free phone consultation - no credit card, no intake fee, just a call to establish whether you're a reasonable candidate and to answer questions before you drive anywhere. From there, you'll need to schedule an in-person physical exam at either the Jupiter or Fort Lauderdale location. This isn't a formality. Testosterone is a Schedule III controlled substance, and NovaGenix requires the physical evaluation that many fully remote platforms have been criticized for skipping.

Labs are included in the program and handled as part of your initial workup. You're not mailing in a finger-prick kit; blood is drawn through the clinic or a local draw site. Once results are back and Dr. Mackey sets a treatment plan, medications ship directly to you, so ongoing therapy doesn't require constant office visits just to restock.

Follow-up monitoring - repeat bloodwork to check levels, hematocrit, and other markers - should be part of any responsibly managed TRT program. Ask during your consultation what the cadence looks like and whether follow-up labs carry an additional cost.

## Pricing

Public pricing isn't disclosed; you'll get a quote after the intake process. That's common among physician-run hormone clinics, but it makes comparison shopping harder. Given the in-person exam requirement and the physician-only prescribing model, expect costs to run higher than the subscription-tier telehealth platforms. What you're paying for is more clinical contact and a physician with genuine depth in this specialty - which has real value, depending on what you're looking for. Come into the phone consultation with specific questions about program fees, visit costs, and what follow-up care looks like financially.

Whether insurance covers any portion, or whether FSA/HSA accounts can be applied, isn't stated publicly. Worth asking directly.

## What's included

Labs are bundled into the program, covering the baseline bloodwork needed to diagnose and then monitor therapy. Treatment options include testosterone injections, topical gel, and HCG - a broader menu than many lower-cost platforms that offer injections only. The inclusion of HCG matters for patients concerned about testicular atrophy or fertility preservation while on TRT. Medications are shipped to your home after the initial evaluation, which removes the inconvenience of picking up prescriptions in person on an ongoing basis.

## Pros and cons

Dr. Mackey's experience is the strongest argument for NovaGenix. Twenty years focused on TRT means he has managed the edge cases - the patients who don't respond predictably, the ones who need protocol adjustments, the ones with complicating factors. That kind of clinical depth is genuinely hard to find on platforms where a nurse practitioner reviews your labs remotely. The free phone consultation is also a low-pressure way to get real information before committing any time or money.

The limitations are geographic and financial. NovaGenix serves Florida only, and even within Florida, you need to physically get to Jupiter or Fort Lauderdale for your exam. If you're in Orlando, Tampa, or the Panhandle, that's a meaningful trip. The pricing opacity is a recurring frustration - without any public ballpark, it's hard to know whether the program fits your budget before you've already invested time in consultations. For patients who have done their research and specifically want an in-person physician-led clinic, those tradeoffs are manageable. For patients who want convenience first, they aren't.

## Bottom line

NovaGenix is built for a specific kind of patient: someone in South Florida who wants a physician with serious TRT experience to evaluate them in person before prescribing. The model is more conservative than the fully virtual platforms, which is a feature for patients who want that level of clinical scrutiny. The in-person requirement, the Florida-only footprint, and the undisclosed pricing mean it won't work for most people searching online - but for the right patient in the right zip code, it's a credible choice.
